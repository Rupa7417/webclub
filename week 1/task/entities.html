SPECIAL CHARECTERS & SYMBOLS IN HTML

In HTML, certain characters are reserved and must be represented by character entities.
Here are some common special characters and symbols in HTML:

  • ©

    • Copyright symbol

  • <

    • Less Than Symbol

  • >

    • Greater Than Symbol

  • &

    • Ampersand

  • €

    • Euro currency symbol

  • ™

    • Trademark symbol
